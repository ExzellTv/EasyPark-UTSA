How to Edit Parking Masks

The mask editor allows you to define parking spot boundaries on your video feed. Follow these steps to configure your parking lot layout.

Getting Started

Run the mask creator tool from your terminal:

python3 tools/mask_creator.py
1

Draw Parking Spots

Left-click and drag to draw rectangular bounding boxes around each parking spot in your video feed.

2

Save Your Work

Press S to save all parking spots to data/spots.json

3

Delete Mistakes

Press D to delete the most recently added parking spot.

4

Reset All Spots

Press R to clear all parking spots and start fresh.

5

Exit the Editor

Press ESC to close the mask editor when you're finished.

Important

After saving your parking spot definitions, you must restart the FastAPI server for the changes to take effect:

uvicorn main:app --reload
